About Us

Crafting excellence from India's textile capital

Welcome to Kevalam Ethnic Wear, where rich Indian heritage meets modern craftsmanship.

Based in Surat, the textile hub of India, we are a leading manufacturer specializing in premium Sherwanis, Indo-Western wear, Jodhpuri suits, and a comprehensive range of high-quality garment fabrics.

We pride ourselves on sourcing the finest materials and utilizing precision production techniques to create standout attire for the modern groom and fashion-forward individuals.

A Humble Beginning, A Grand Vision

Kevalam is born from a simple belief: that every man deserves to feel distinguished on his most important days. We focus on precise tailoring, rich fabrics, and silhouettes that honor tradition.
